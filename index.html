About Us

Couture Closet is all about helping you express yourself through fashion. We offer a blend of stylish, high-quality clothing and accessories that are perfect for everyday wear or making a fashion statement. Our focus is on creating pieces that feel unique and timeless while being mindful of sustainability. At Couture Closet, we believe your wardrobe should tell a story, and we're here to make that happen!

 

 

 

Aria Montgomery

Head of Operations

Aria is the strategic innovator behind Couture Closet's smooth operations. As the Head of Operations, she is concerned with supply chains, managing budgets, and driving the commitment of the brand to sustainability. With a detail-oriented approach and skilled in problem-solving and decision making, Aria ensures that every department operates with complete efficiency, making Couture Closet a leader and at the top in ethical and sustainable fashion.

Spencer Hastings

Engagement Director

Spencer serves as the Director of Client Engagement at Couture Closet, building meaningful connections with clients and partners. With expertise in fashion advising and event coordination, they create unforgettable shopping experiences that bring the brand to life. From managing collaborations to engaging the community through social media, Spencer ensures that Couture Closet remains synonymous with elegance and offers customer-centric service.

Lily Van Der Woodson

Creative Director

As the Creative Director of Couture Closet, Lily leads the charge in shaping the brand's unique and innovate designs. With a background in fashion and a passion for artistic expression, she transforms bold ideas into trendsetting collections. Mixing modern with timelessness, Known for blending modern styles with timeless elegance, Lily fuses pieces together into items that showcase individuality and empowers customers to express themselves through fashion.
